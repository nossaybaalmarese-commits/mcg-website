About MCG

Mediterranee Commerce Global SARL (MCG) is a Morocco-based international trading company headquartered in Nador. Located in the industrial zone of Selouane, the company operates at a strategic point that enables efficient national distribution and global trade connectivity.

MCG is built on strong foundations of professionalism, reliability, and operational efficiency. With a registered capital of 100,000 DHS, the company provides trusted solutions in the import and distribution sector.

Our Activities

  • Import & Export of goods
  • Distribution of mineral water
  • Food product trading
  • Cleaning and hygiene products supply

Our Vision

We aim to build long-term partnerships by delivering consistent quality, competitive pricing, and efficient logistics solutions tailored to international markets.
